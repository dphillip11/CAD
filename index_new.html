Camera Controls:

  • Left Mouse Button + Drag: Orbit camera around the model
  • Middle Mouse Button + Drag: Pan camera
  • Scroll Wheel: Zoom in/out

Selection & Editing:

  • Right Click: Select a face (face will be highlighted)
  • Scroll Wheel (when face selected): Extrude/translate the selected face along its normal

Undo/Redo:

  • Ctrl+Z (or Cmd+Z on Mac): Undo last operation
  • Ctrl+Shift+Z (or Cmd+Shift+Z on Mac): Redo operation
  • Or use the Undo and Redo buttons above

Debug Mode:

  • Click Debug button to toggle debug visualization
